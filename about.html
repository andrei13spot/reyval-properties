About Reyval Properties

Your Trusted Partner in Real Estate Excellence

At Reyval Properties, we bring trust, expertise, and a passion for luxury living to every real estate journey. Your goals, our commitment.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional real estate services that exceed expectations, creating lasting relationships built on trust, transparency, and outstanding results for every client.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and innovative real estate company in Los Angeles, known for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction.

Our Story

Founded by John Andrei Nañola, Reyval Properties began with a simple vision: to transform the real estate experience through direct property ownership and investment. What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted name in Los Angeles real estate, serving hundreds of satisfied clients with personalized, professional service.

Our Growth

From our humble beginnings, we've expanded our property portfolio to include residential sales, commercial properties, investment opportunities, and property management. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing personalized service tailored to each client's unique needs.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction are the cornerstones of our business. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust and delivering results that exceed expectations.
